# Adding the Shade America Portal to Your Phone

Save the team portal to your phone's home screen so it opens with one tap, just like any app.

---

## iPhone (and iPad)

1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone. *This only works in Safari* — Chrome on iPhone won't do it right.
2. Go to **shadeamerica.team** and log in.
3. Tap the **Share button** at the bottom of the screen — the square with an arrow pointing up.
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. You'll see a preview with the Shade America icon and the name "SA Portal." Tap **Add** in the top-right.
6. Done. The icon is on your home screen. Tap it to log in like any other app.

---

## Android (Samsung, Pixel, Motorola, etc.)

1. Open **Chrome** on your phone.
2. Go to **shadeamerica.team** and log in.
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top-right corner of Chrome.
4. Tap **Add to Home screen** (some phones say "Install app" — either works).
5. Confirm the name and tap **Add** or **Install**.
6. If your phone asks "Add automatically" vs. "tap and drag," choose **Add automatically**.
7. Done. Tap the icon to open the portal.

---

## Tips

- **Stay logged in.** Check "Remember me" the first time you log in so you don't have to retype your password every time.
- **Wrong icon showing?** Pull down on the page to refresh, then redo the Add to Home Screen flow — your phone may have cached an old icon.
- **Removing the icon:** long-press it and tap Remove (or drag to the trash). Removes only the shortcut, not your account.
